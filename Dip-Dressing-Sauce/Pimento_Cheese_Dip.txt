Pimento Cheese Dip

sharp cheddar - 16 oz
monteray jack or medium cheddar - 16 oz
cream cheese - 8 oz
powdered sugar - 1 t
cayenne pepper - 1 t
mayonaise - 1 T
pimentos, diced, no not drain - 7 oz

bring cream cheese to room temperature
mix cream cheese, mayonaise, sugar, cayenne, and pimentos
grate cheddar and monteray jack cheese
stir together
