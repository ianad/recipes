Ziti with Bacon and Two Cheeses


ziti rigati, uncooked		3 cups (8 oz)
bacon, diced		6 slices
red pepper, thin strips	1 medium (1 cup)
onion, chopped		1/2 cup
spinach, w/o stems, chopped 	5 cups (5 oz)
fontina cheese, shredded	1 cup (4 oz)
gorgonzola cheese, crumbled	1/2 cup (2 oz)
butter, cut into pieces	1/4 cup
walnuts or pine nuts, toasted	1/4 cup
chives, chopped		2 T

To toast walnuts, lay on microwave-safe plate.  Microwave at high power 2 to 4 minutes 
or until lightly browned, stirring after each minute.

Cook pasta and drain.
Cook bacon until crisp.  Save grease.
Over medium-high heat, saute red pepper and onion in 1 tablespoon bacon grease 
in a 5 quart saucepan until tender, about 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.
Add spinach and cook until spinach wilts, about 1 minute.
Remove from heat, stir in hot pasta.
Stir in bacon, cheeses, and butter.
Toss until cheese melts.
Garnish with toasted walnuts and chives.

Six 1 cup servings.
