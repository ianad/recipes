Cannellini Beans and Tomatoes

tomatoes, medium, chopped	4
red pepper, crushed		1 tsp
garlic, minced		2 tsp
basil, chopped		3/4 cup
salt			1 tsp
pepper			1/2 tsp
olive oil			1/4 cup

cannellini (white kidney) beans	2 - 15 oz. cans
water			1/2 cup

parmesan cheese, shredded, 	1/2 cup 
fontina cheese, diced	1/2 lb.


Combine tomatoes, red pepper, garlic, basil, salt, pepper, and olive oil in a small bowl.
Allow to stand at room temperature, stirring occasionally, for 2-3 hours.  (optional)

Pour Cannellini beans and their liquid into a saucepan, 
add water, 
heat through, covered, over med-low heat.  
drain beans and rinse under hot water.

Transfer beans to a serving bowl.  
Add cheeses and toss gently until the cheeses begin to melt.  
Mix in tomato mixture.  
Serve warm or at room temperature. 
serves 4-6
