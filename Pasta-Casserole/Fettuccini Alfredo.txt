Fettuccini Alfredo

Fettuccini, 8 oz dry or 9 oz fresh
butter, softened, 1/3 cup
whipping cream, room temperature, 3/4 cup
Parmesan cheese, freshly grated, 3/4 cup (2 oz)
salt, 1/2 teaspoon
white pepper, 1/8 teaspoon
nutmeg, 1/8 teaspoon

Cook fettuccini, drain, remove pan from heat, or butter will separate.
Combine hot fettuccini and butter, toss to coat.
Add half the cream, toss well.
Add half the Parmesan cheese, toss well.
Add remaining cream, toss well.
Add remaining Parmesan cheese, salt, white pepper, and nutmeg, toss well.
Serve immediately in warm serving dish with salt, pepper and parmesan cheese.
