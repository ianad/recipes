Couscous with Spinach

olive oil, 1/3 cup
garlic, 4 cloves
onion, 2 medium
pine nuts, 1/2 cup

tomatoes, 28 oz can, chopped and drained, reserve 1/2 cup juice

tomatoe juice, 1/2 cup (reserved from tomatoes above)
basil, 6 leaves, 1 1/2 T minced
spinach, 1 10 oz package
pepper	

vegetable stock, 1.5 cup (14.5 oz can), or water
couscous, 1 cup
salt, 1 t

muenster cheese, 2 cups (1/2 lb)



Preheat oven to 375 degrees.

Heat olive oil in large skillet.
Saute the garlic, onion, and pine nuts.
Add tomatoes, and cook 10 minutes more, stirring frequently.
Mix in the reserved tomato juice, basil, spinach, and pepper.

bring vegestable stock to boil
combine couscous, boiling stock, and salt in large bowl, 
cover with plate, 
sit 5 minutes, 
fluff with fork.

Spread the couscous mixture in a 9x13 baking dish.  
Spread the tomato mixture on the couscous.  
Sprinkle with cheese.  
Cover the dish with foil and bake 25 minutes, or until hot and bubbly.
