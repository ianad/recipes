
PUMPKIN BREAD

sugar		1 1/2 cups 
oil		1/2 cup 
eggs		2 
pumpkin 		1 cup (1/2 can)
water		1/3 cup 
salt		3/4 tsp. 
cinnamon	1/2 tsp. 
nutmeg		1/2 tsp. 
baking soda	1 tsp 
flour		1 2/3 cups 

Mix together. Pour into loaf pan. Bake 1 hour at 350 degrees. 
Test for doneness with toothpick. It should come out clean.
