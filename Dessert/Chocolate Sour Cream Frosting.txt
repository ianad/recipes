chocolate morsels, semisweet	1 cup
butter			1/4 cup
sour cream		1/2 cup
vanilla			1 tsp
powdered sugar		2 1/2 cups
salt			1/4 tsp

melt chocolate and buter over low heat, stirring frequently
cool 10 minutes
stir in sour cream, vanilla, and salt
gradually add powdered sugar
beat by hand untill smooth
frosts two 9 inch layers
