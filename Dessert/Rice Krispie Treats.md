Kellogg's® Rice Krispies Treats®

butter or margarine - 3 tablespoons  
marshmallows - 1 package (10 oz. about 40)
Rice Krispies cereal  - 6 cups 

Melt butter in large saucepan over low heat. 
Add marshmallows and stir until completely melted. 
Add Rice Krispies cereal. Stir until well coated.  
Coat 13 x 9 x 2-inch pan with cooking spray. 
Press mixture evenly into pan Using buttered spatula.
Cut into squares when cool. Best if served the same day.
