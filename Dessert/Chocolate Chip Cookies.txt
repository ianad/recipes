Chocolate Chip Cookies

butter, softened		1 cup (2 sticks)
sugar			3/4 c
brown sugar		3/4 c

eggs			2
vanilla extract		1 t

flour			2 1/4 c
baking soda		1 t
salt			1 t

choc chips, semi-sweet	12 oz package
nuts, chopped		1 c

Preheat oven to 375 deg,
Beat butter, sugar, and brown sugar until creamy.
Beat in eggs and vanilla.
Sift or stir together flour, baking soda and salt.
Add flour mixture; mix well. 
Stir in chocolate morsels and nuts.
Drop by rounded teaspoonfuls, 2 inches apart, onto ungreased baking sheets.
Bake 9 to 11 minutes or until golden brown.
Cool on baking sheet for 2 minutes
Remove to wire racks.
Makes about 5 dozen.
