Peanut Butter Pie

Peanut butter	1/2 cup
Cream Cheese	8 oz package
Cool Whip	8 oz bucket
Condensed milk	14 oz can

Mix peanut butter and softened cream cheese
Slowly add condensed milk
Slowly add cool whip
Pour mixture into crust
Freeze
Serve with chocolate sauce dribbled on top

Use pie crust or graham cracker crust

Graham cracker crust:

	graham crackers, 7
	butter, 3 T
	
	crumble graham crackers
	add melted butter
	press into pan
	cook at 400 deg for 10 minutes
