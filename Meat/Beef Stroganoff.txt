Beef Stroganoff

beef, boneless, 1 lb, cut into 1 inch cubes

mushrooms, sliced, 1/2 lb.
onions, 1 large
butter, 1/4 cup

salt, 1/2 t
pepper, 1/2 t
parsley, 1 t
paprika, 1 t
thyme, 1/4 t
bay leaf, 1

cream cheese, 4 oz 
dry red wine, 1/4 cup

In large pot: 
Saute, beef, onions, and mushrooms in butter
Add other dry ingredients.
Add cream cheese and wine.  
Cover and simmer 1/2 hour, stirring occasionally.
Serve over rice.  Serves 2.
