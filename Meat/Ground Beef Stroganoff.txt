
Ground Beef Stroganoff

ground beef		1 lb
onion			1
garlic salt		1/2 tsp
pepper			1/4 tsp
cream of mushroom soup	1 can condensed
mushrooms		4 oz sliced
sour cream		3/4 cup

In medium skillet, bround ground beef and onion.
Add garlic slat, pepper, soup, and mushrooms.
Simmer covered, 20 minutes.
Stir in sour cream, heat but do not boil.

Serve over egg noodles or rice.  Serves 4.
