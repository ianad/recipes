

Bourbon Chicken

chicken cut up	2	3 lbs		4.5	
cooking oil	1	2 T		3
garlic		1 	2 clove		3
ginger		1/4 	1/2 t		3/4
cayenne pepper	1 	1 1/2 t		2

brown sugar	1/3 	1/2 cup		3/4
soy sauce		1/3 	1/2 cup		3/4
bourbon		1 	1 1/2  cup		2 1/4

Brown chicken in cooking oil with garlic, ginger, and cayenne pepper.

Add brown sugar, soy sauce, and bourbon.

Heat to boil, until reduced.

Serves 4/6, serve with rice.
