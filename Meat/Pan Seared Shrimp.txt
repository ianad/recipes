Pan Seared Shrimp

butter			3 Tbsp
parsley			2 Tbsp
lemon juice		1 Tbsp
garlic			1 clove

shrimp (21/25 count)	1 1/2 lb
salt			1/4 tsp
pepper			1/4 tsp
sugar			1/8 tsp

vegetable oil		2 Tbsp


Beat butter with fork in small bowl until light and fluffy.
Stir in parsley, lemon juice, and garlic. 

Peel and devein the shrimp.
Toss shrimp, salt, pepper, and sugar in medium bowl.

Heat half the oil in 12-inch slillet over high heat until smoking.
Add half the shrimp to skillet in single layer and cook until spotty brown and edges turn pink, about 1 minute.
Remove skillet from heat.
Flip each shrimp with tongs and let stand until all but the very center is opaque, about 30 seconds.
Transfer shrimp to large plate.
Repeat with the other half of oil and shrimp.

Return first batch to skillet along with garlic-lemon butter and toss to combine.  
Cover skillet and let stand until shrimp are cooked through, about 1-2 minutes.

Serve with lemon wedges if desired.
