Spice Rub for Pork or Beef 

salt		4 Tbsp  
paprika		2 Tbsp 
chili powder	2 Tbsp
garlic powder	2 Tbsp  
brown sugar	2 Tbsp 
cayenne pepper	1 Tbsp 
ground cumin	1 Tbsp 
dry mustard	1 Tbsp 
black pepper	1 Tbsp 
sage 		1 tsp 
oregano		1 tsp 

Yield: Makes about 1 cup 

Let the meat marinate in the rub for two hours at 
room temperature or overnight in the refrigerator.
